About Me

My name is Tri Juniyanti, a dedicated Manual & Automation Quality Assurance Engineer with over 4 years of hands-on experience ensuring the quality of digital banking, financial technology, and enterprise-level applications.

Throughout my career, I have contributed to major banking projects in Indonesia— including mobile banking, corporate banking, CMS, AS/400 core systems, BNPL, and digital onboarding. Working in high-stakes and regulated environments has strengthened my precision, analytical skills, and commitment to delivering defect-free releases.

I specialize in:

  • Test Planning & Strategy for complex financial systems
  • Functional, Regression & Integration Testing
  • API Testing (Postman, Swagger)
  • UAT Coordination & Communication with Users
  • Defect Analysis & End-to-End Scenario Design
  • Automation (currently developing with Selenium & Python)

What drives me is the challenge of understanding how systems work behind the scenes and ensuring every feature works exactly as users expect. I enjoy breaking things, finding edge cases, and improving user experience through clean and structured test scenarios.

Outside of QA, I love sepatu roda, exploring new technology tools, and continuously learning modern automation frameworks. I am curious, detail-oriented, and fully committed to growing into a global QA professional.
